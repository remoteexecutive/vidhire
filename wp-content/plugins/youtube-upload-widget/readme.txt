The YouTube Upload Widget allows you to easily add video to your WordPress blog.

== Description ==
The YouTube Upload Widget adds video to your blogpost!  You can now create a video through your webcam, upload it directly to your own YouTube channel, and share it in your post without ever leaving Wordpress.  All the work is done securely through the [YouTube APIs](https://developers.google.com/youtube/youtube_upload_widget).

= Features =

* Upload directly to YouTube using your webcam without leaving WordPress.
* Preview webcam video or other videos before posting.
* Search for your favorite video to add without leaving the new post page.

== Installation ==
1. Install YouTube Upload Widget either via the WordPress.org plugins directory, or by uploading the files to your server (in the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory).
2. After activating the plugin, you will see a YouTube button above the post editor on the new post and the edit post screens.
